About Tita PLC

Founded in Desē, Amhara Region — built on precision manufacturing, Ethiopian craftsmanship, and a global vision.

Our Story

From a Single Loom
to Global Exports

Tita PLC was founded in 2008 with a single extrusion line and a vision to supply quality polypropylene packaging to Ethiopian agriculture and industry.

Today, we operate a 14,000m² facility in Desē, Amhara Region, housing 12 weaving lines, 4 extrusion lines, and 3 rotogravure printing presses.

Our products reach buyers across 18+ countries including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kenya, Germany, and India — supporting agriculture, retail, and industrial packaging needs.
