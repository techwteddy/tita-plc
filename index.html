Why Tita PLC

End-to-End Manufacturing
& Logistics Excellence

🏭

In-House Manufacturing

State-of-the-art extrusion, weaving, and printing lines producing 2,400+ tons monthly with full quality control.

🎨

Custom Printing & Branding

Up to 6-color BOPP lamination and gravure printing. Your brand on every bag, exactly as designed.

Certified Quality
